You are back from the dead, but wait, hold on, who are you again?

Lydia Was Here is a game about a botched resurrection, where you have to pretend to be someone you're pretty sure you're not.

Navigate your friendships and protect your secret. Will you escape or embrace your new role?

Fully Hand-Drawn Visuals

Every asset is drawn by us!!!

Psychological Horror Focused on Delusions

A horror rooted in silence, absence, and emotional decay
rather than jumpscares.

A Living Nightmare

Explore Lydia’s family home as your mind changes over
time.

Fragment of Reality

Navigate surreal dream spaces where environments reflect Lydia’s
mental state.

A Friend Group on the Edge

Experience the story through the perspectives of friends struggling
with guilt, denial, and the cost of resurrection.

Classic RPG Maker Exploration
